Mixing & Mastering

When individuals approach us with completed songs, in addition to the recording and mixing process, we offer our expertise in enhancing your music by incorporating new elements such as synths and post-production effects. Our goal is to help your music reach its full potential.

Alternatively, you're welcome to bring us your song concepts or guitar sketches. We'll assist you in crafting the arrangement and meticulously record all the instruments and vocals necessary to bring your vision to life.

Please note that we do not provide services for "backing tracks" or "gift songs." However, if you have a creative concept in mind, we can collaborate with you to produce a unique cover song in your preferred style.

Our primary objective is to raise awareness about Latvian musicians and share their talent with as many listeners as possible."
