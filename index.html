Recording

When people come to us with finished songs, in addition to the recording and mixing itself, we can help with adding new parts, synths, and post-production effects so that your music opens up to its maximum.

Also, you can only come to us with song ideas or guitar sketches. We will help you write the arrangement and record all the instruments and vocals.

We do not record “backing tracks” or “gift songs”, but we can help make a cover song in your style if a creative approach is implied.

We aim to increase awareness about Latvian musicians for as many listeners as possible.
